What is Tree Console?

Tree Consoles is a Polish company passionate about crafting retro-inspired consoles with a modern twist. Like our friends at Pączek Studio, we focus on innovation, avoiding direct copies of classic designs. Powered by Raspberry Pi, our consoles are created for the love of gaming and are not intended for commercial sale.
